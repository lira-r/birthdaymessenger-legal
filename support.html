Common Questions

How do I import contacts?

Tap "Add contact" → "Import" tab. Birthly will request permission to read your phone's contacts so you can pick which ones to add. Only the contacts you select are saved to your Birthly account — the rest stay on your device.

How do credits work?

Each AI-generated birthday message uses 1 credit. New accounts receive 3 free welcome credits valid for 30 days. Paid credit packs are valid for 365 days from purchase.

How do I get a refund?

All in-app purchases are processed by Apple. Refund requests must be submitted to Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Why didn't I get a birthday notification?

Make sure notifications are enabled in iOS Settings → Birthly → Notifications, and that the master toggle in Birthly's Settings → Notifications is on.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → scroll to Critical Actions → Delete account. This permanently removes your data from our servers.
